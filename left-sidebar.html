Is coaching for me?

  • You have a goal but you don't know how to achieve it.
  • You are dissatisfied with your current situation and would like to make changes. You want to be challenged to fulfil your potential.
  • You want to overcome limiting beliefs and develop a positive mindset.
  • You want to find creative solutions to the challenges you are currently facing.
  • You want to feel a greater sense of fulfilment and well being.

What will I get from coaching?

  • You will gain clarity, learn how to set goals and prioritise what's really important in your life.
  • You will discover how to get from where you are to where you want to be as together we design an achieveable, tailormade action plan. You will gain an enhanced awareness of how your thoughts, words and beliefs impact your experience of life.
  • You will be held accountable through regular coaching sessions and check-ins.
  • You will have regular opportunities to discuss ideas and be challenged to grow out of your comfort zone.
  • Your motivation will grow as you see and chart your progress.
  • You will have an opportunity to focus on your personal development.

What happens in a coaching session?

Coaching sessions take the form of a structured conversation. We start out by identifying your long term goals and then map out the key milestones on the journey. Each session will focus on creating an action plan to get to the next milestone. Along the way we will delve deep to uncover what's been holding you back and uncover the all strenghts and resources you have available to achieve your goals. The process of coaching empowers you to become the creator of your life by shifting your mindset to one that is productive and constructive, and giving the tools to methodically realise your dreams.
